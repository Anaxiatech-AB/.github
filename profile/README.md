## 🏢 About Anaxiatech AB

Anaxiatech AB is a Swedish technology company specializing in artificial intelligence solutions for infrastructure monitoring and analysis. Our flagship product, **Crackz**, leverages advanced computer vision and machine learning to detect structural anomalies in harbors and marine infrastructure.

## 🎯 Our Mission

To enhance infrastructure safety and longevity through cutting-edge AI technology, providing actionable insights that help maintain critical infrastructure before problems become catastrophic.

## 🚀 Products & Solutions

### [Crackz](https://github.com/Anaxiatech-AB/crackz) - AI-Powered Crack Detection
- **Advanced Computer Vision**: PyTorch Lightning-based segmentation models
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Both CLI and GUI applications available
- **Flexible Deployment**: Docker support with CPU/GPU variants
- **Professional Workflow**: Structured data pipeline with train/val/test splits

**Key Features:**
- Real-time crack detection in harbor infrastructure
- Batch processing for large-scale analysis
- Customizable training for specific environments
- Export capabilities for reporting and documentation
